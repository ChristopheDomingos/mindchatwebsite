COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MindChat products, ordering, and support.

What's included with my MindChat Pro purchase?

Each MindChat Pro purchase includes the EEG headset, a protective charging case, USB-C charging cable, quick start guide, and access to the MindChat mobile app. Bundle purchases include additional software licenses and services as specified in the bundle description.

Do I need a subscription to use the MindChat Pro?

The MindChat Pro headset includes basic functionality through our free mobile app. However, to access advanced features like AI-powered insights, continuous monitoring, and specialized assessments, a software subscription is required. All bundles include the first year of subscription in the purchase price.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all hardware purchases. If you're not completely satisfied with your MindChat Pro headset, you can return it within 30 days of receipt for a full refund. Software subscriptions are non-refundable once activated. Bundle purchases may be returned within 30 days, provided all items are returned in their original condition.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the continental United States. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout. International shipping times vary by destination but generally range from 7-14 business days. All shipments include tracking information that will be emailed to you once your order is processed.

Can I purchase additional licenses for my bundle?

Yes, additional software licenses can be purchased separately for any bundle. Contact our sales team for volume pricing on additional licenses. We also offer custom enterprise solutions for organizations requiring more than 25 licenses or special integrations with existing systems.
